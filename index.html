Introduction

-For the final project, I employed a emission data available from National Emissions Inventory (NEI), "http://d396qusza40orc.cloudfront.net/exdata%2Fdata%2FNEI_data.zip". The NEI emissions is compiled by the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) with the aim to track emission in the atmosphere.

-In this analysis we focus in the visual analsys of emission of four type of vehicles (Non-road, Nonpoint, On-road, and Point) in four years (1999, 2002, 2005, and 2008).
